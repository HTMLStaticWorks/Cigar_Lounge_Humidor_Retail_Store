Knowledge Base

Common questions about our curated tobacco collection and lounge etiquette.

How should I store my cigars if I don't have a humidor?

For short-term storage, a Ziploc bag with a damp paper towel (not touching the cigars) or a Boveda pack can work. However, for any collection intended to last more than a week, we strongly recommend a cedar-lined, climate-controlled humidor to maintain flavor and prevent drying.

What is the best way to cut a cigar?

A clean, quick cut is essential. Whether using a straight guillotine, a V-cutter, or a punch, ensure you only remove the very top of the cap. Cutting too deep can cause the wrapper to unravel. Our staff is always available to demonstrate proper technique.

Do you offer locker rentals for members?

Yes, our Connoisseur and Private Reserve membership tiers include access to personal, humidified locker slots. These lockers are monitored for temperature and humidity 24/7 to ensure your private collection stays in peak condition.
