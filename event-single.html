Exclusive Masterclass

Bourbon & Broadleaf: A Deep Dive

May 15, 2026 7:00 PM - 9:30 PM The Reserve Suite

Join our resident sommelier and humidor manager for an evening dedicated to the rich, earthy complexity of broadleaf wrappers. Often misunderstood but highly prized by connoisseurs, the broadleaf wrapper provides the backbone for some of the world's most legendary cigars.

What to Expect

  • A tasting flight of three premium Nicaraguan broadleaf cigars.
  • Pairing sessions with four aged Kentucky straight bourbons.
  • Detailed discussion on the fermentation process of broadleaf tobacco.
  • Complimentary cedar spill lighting for all participants.
